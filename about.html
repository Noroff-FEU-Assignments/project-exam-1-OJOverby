About Me

Hello, fellow adventurers! I'm Alex Sterling, the face and spirit behind the Tenacious Traveler. Welcome to my corner of the internet, where I share tales from the road less traveled, captured moments of breathtaking beauty, and the lessons learned from each bend and break in the path.

My Journey

Born and raised in the shadow of the Rocky Mountains in Boulder, Colorado, I was fortunate enough to have adventure right on my doorstep. My childhood was filled with hikes, camping trips, and an ever-growing curiosity about the world beyond those majestic peaks. With a degree in Environmental Science and a minor in Creative Writing, I've found a way to blend my love for the natural world with my passion for storytelling.

After college, I strapped on my backpack, grabbed my camera, and set out to explore the corners of the globe. From the ancient forests of the Amazon to the snowy peaks of the Himalayas, each destination has been a teacher, imparting lessons of resilience, humility, and the interconnectedness of our world.

Why Tenacious Traveler?

This blog is more than just a travel diary. It's a testament to the power of persistence, the beauty of the natural world, and the boundless discoveries that await when we step outside our comfort zones. "Tenacious" reflects my approach to both travel and life – embracing challenges, persevering through setbacks, and always moving forward with an open heart and an insatiable curiosity.

My Mission

Through the Tenacious Traveler, I aim to inspire you to embark on your own adventures, whether that's a solo hike in your local forest or a journey to a distant land. Here, you'll find stories of adventure and misadventure, tips for sustainable travel, and photography that captures the awe-inspiring beauty of our planet.

But beyond the thrills and the vistas, I hope to foster a deeper appreciation for our world and its cultures. I believe that travel, when done respectfully and mindfully, can be a powerful force for good, promoting understanding, conservation, and a shared sense of humanity.

Let's Connect

Your stories, questions, and insights are what make this community vibrant. I invite you to join the conversation—share your own tales of adventure, ask questions, or simply say hello. You can reach me through the comments, on social media, or by sending a carrier pigeon to the nearest mountaintop.

Together, let's embrace the tenacity within us and venture forth into the great unknown, for it's there that we find ourselves truly alive.

Happy travels,
