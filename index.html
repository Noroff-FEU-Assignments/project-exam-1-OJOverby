Who is Tenacious Traveler

Meet Alex Sterling, the spirited voice behind the Tenacious Traveler blog. Born in Boulder, Colorado, with the Rocky Mountains as his first playground, Alex cultivated a deep love for adventure and the natural world. With a degree in Environmental Science and a minor in Creative Writing, he expertly combines his passions, sharing captivating stories and insights from his global travels. From the Amazon forests to the Himalayas, Alex's journey is a testament to the power of persistence and the beauty of our planet. Through the Tenacious Traveler, Alex aims to inspire others to explore responsibly, embrace challenges, and foster a deeper appreciation for the interconnectedness of our world. Join him in embracing the spirit of adventure and the tenacity within each of us to discover the great unknown.
